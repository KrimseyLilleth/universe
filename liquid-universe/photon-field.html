Liquid Universe

The field — a frictionless 3D lattice of beads, packed solid. There is no empty space.

A photon is not a thing. It is one bead-width of displacement traveling through. When a bead is knocked loose, the next bead falls in behind it. That propagating void is the photon.

Behind the displacement, a torus wake forms — a smoke ring trailing the flow. The wake is the same shape as the universe, recursing all the way down.

One photon

A single photon travels through the bead lattice. Behind it, a torus wake spins up — visible as the trailing ring of poloidal flow. The lattice beads near the wake are nudged from their rest positions; everywhere else they hold still.
